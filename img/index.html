AIESEC IN EGYPT

MCP WORD

49 years of greatness and impact, of history and of stories, of leadership development and much more is what our entity represents and what it is meant to be.

AIESEC in Egypt. This sentence of three words took a lot of sweat and blood, sleepless nights, commitments, discipline and above all love and ownership to what we do so that this entity can exist and flourish the way we proudly see it nowadays.

6 years ago when joined AIESEC, leading the entity was only a dream for me. And now I confidently say that dreams do come true. AIESEC in Egypt shaped the leader who I am today and gave me to opportunity to impact and develop more leaders for a better world and a better Egypt.

Leading AIESEC in Egypt is an immense privilege and responsibility. A life-changing and one of a kind experience that you will be always grateful for. It takes a lot bravery, determination and consistency to take the decision to lead AIESEC in Egypt and to develop youth through it.

Now is the time for the 50th President of AISEC in Egypt, now is the time for the golden president of our beloved entity. Filled with gratitude and pride, I am wishing the best of luck to the brave leaders out there to lead our beloved AIESEC in Egypt !

Eligibility

NATIONALITY

AGE

MCP Role

  • Planning
    Organize the process of MC planning. Build and implement tracking and decision making systems. Track, review, coach MCVPs on their strategic plans. Support in budgeting and financial decision making.
  • Team Management
    Build and implement management tools and principles. Organize and run MC Team Days. Align and coordinate MCVPs during conferences and events. Run MC weekly Meetings. Ensure that all MCVPs have a correct plan and clear job description. Run HR processes in the MC.
  • Organizational Development
    Build relationship and negotiate with other MCPs about country partnerships. Track organizational goals and indicators. Ensure that conferences are being used correctly. Ensure the alignment of MCVPs with the strategies of LCs development and support. Support the development of strategies for positioning to stakeholders.
  • Internal & External Representation
    Represent AIESEC in Egypt as an entity in events and meetings. Track and manage MC main accounts. Support in product development and in creating strategies for external relations. Internally represent AIESEC in Egypt in global and national plenary and with our main IR partners and alumni body.
  • Governance
    Manage Supervisory Group. Send reports and implement outputs from SG Meetings. Make sure that constitution and compendium are being followed accordingly. Facilitate and participate in CSN meetings. Deliver all requirements for AIESEC in Egypt's membership criteria.

Questionnaire

  • Personal Block (4)

    1- Who are you and what do you stand for? Define 5 keywords that defines you and explain them based on your experiences in life
    2- Why are you standing for MCP 23|24 of AIESEC in Egypt? What will success mean to you as a person? and what will success mean to AIESEC in Egypt on June 30th 2023?
    3- Explain your connection with the organization through all the elements of the AIESEC Way?
    4- What kind of leadership does AIESEC in Egypt need in 23|24? What is your leadership style and how will this affect the entity? How will you lead your team?

  • Global And External Block (5)

    1- What has AIESEC in Egypt done for Egypt so far? How do you envision the impact that AIESEC in Egypt can do for Egypt?
    2- Based on the current context, how can AIESEC in Egypt enhance its presence and positioning in the Egyptian market and the global plenary?
    3- What were the main lessons learned from AIESEC 2020 and how do they relate to the midterm direction, AIESEC 2025?
    4- The world is not the same post-COVID, AIESEC neither. How do you see AIESEC in Egypt needs for the following 5 generations? How A2025 is connected with your vision for AIESEC in Egypt until 2025? What is going to be your term role for that?
    5- Which external trends and recent happenings across the world do you see shaping Egypt? And how can AIESEC in Egypt utilize them or mitigate their risk?

  • Internal block (5)

    1- Present a detailed risk assessment plan for AIESEC in Egypt for the term 23|24, and provide explaination and solution for risk mitigation.
    2- Thinking about your profile, what are going to be the non-negotiables that you will set to choose your MC team? How will your future MC team look like? What is the profile that you want to choose for your MC? And for what are you going to be remembered as a team? Create a possible MC Structure for the MC 22/23. How the structure you are proposing will work for your main ambition as MCP?
    3- What do you think are the needed changes for AIESEC in Egypt post-COVID 19? What will be your role as MCP in this process and how will you safeguard our essence?
    4- What are the current obstacales that hinders AIESEC in Egypt from achieving 5000 exchange? What are your purposed course of actions that shall push the culture of AIESEC in Egypt to achieve 5000 exchnages?
    5- Considering the current scenario we face as organization nowadays, how should we shape the AIESEC in Egypt membership journey? How can we ensure AIESEC in Egypt is creating the leaders our country deserves?
